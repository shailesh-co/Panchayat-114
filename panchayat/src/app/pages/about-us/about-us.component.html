About Us

Buldhana Panchayat Samiti is a local self-government institution located in Buldhana Taluka, Buldhana District, Maharashtra. We are committed to improving the quality of life of rural citizens through rural development, social welfare, and local governance.

History

Buldhana Panchayat Samiti was established in 1962 under the Panchayati Raj system in Maharashtra. Since then, we have consistently contributed to the development of infrastructure, education, health, and agriculture in rural areas. Our efforts have enabled many villages in Buldhana Taluka to reach new heights of development.

Objectives
  • Develop infrastructure in rural areas, such as roads, water supply, and sanitation.
  • Improve the quality of life through education and healthcare services.
  • Increase farmers' income by promoting agriculture and allied activities.
  • Implement special schemes for the empowerment of women and girls.
  • Ensure the benefits of government schemes reach villagers directly.
Services

Buldhana Panchayat Samiti provides the following services:

  • Certificates: Birth, death, and residence certificates.
  • Social Security: Old-age pensions, widow pensions, and disability welfare schemes.
  • Healthcare: Primary health centers and vaccination campaigns.
  • Education: Management of Z.P. schools and scholarship schemes.
  • Rural Development: Employment generation and infrastructure projects under MGNREGA.
