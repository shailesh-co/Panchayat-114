Government Schemes

Scheme Name Description Official Portal Link
Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) A national mission to provide banking services to all households, including opening bank accounts, debit cards, insurance, and overdraft facilities. PMJDY Portal
Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) Provides financial assistance to build affordable houses for the poor, especially for EWS and LIG categories in rural and urban areas. PMAY Portal
Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PM-JAY) Offers financial protection for secondary and tertiary healthcare services, providing up to 5 lakh rupees per family for free treatment. PM-JAY Portal
Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) A scheme for the sustainable development of the fisheries sector, focusing on fish production, infrastructure, and employment generation. PMMSY Portal
Swachh Bharat Abhiyan A national campaign to promote cleanliness and sanitation, including ending open defecation and improving waste management. Swachh Bharat Portal
PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana Provides 300 units of free electricity per month to 1 crore households through rooftop solar panels, promoting savings and green energy. PM Surya Ghar Portal
Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGA) Guarantees at least 100 days of wage employment per year to rural households, improving their livelihoods. MGNREGA Portal
Beti Bachao Beti Padhao A scheme to promote the education and protection of girls, reducing gender discrimination and empowering girls. BBBP Portal
Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) Ensures food security for the poor by providing free food grains monthly, initially launched during the COVID-19 pandemic. PMGKAY Portal
Maharashtra State Rural Livelihoods Mission (MSRLM) Focuses on poverty alleviation and livelihood improvement in rural Maharashtra through self-help groups. MSRLM Portal
